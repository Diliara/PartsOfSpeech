Definition: a modifying word that determines the kind of reference a noun or noun group has. Determiners include the following common types:

  • Articles: a/an, the
  • Demonstratives: this, that, these, those
  • Possessives: my, your, his, her, its, our, their, x’s (possessive ’s)
  • Quantifiers: (a) few, fewer, (a) little, many, much, more, most, some, any, etc.
  • Numbers: one, two, three, etc.
